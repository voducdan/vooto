## Bibliographic details

- **Title**: *IELTS Academic Vocabulary: Master 3000+ Academic Vocabularies By Topics Explained In 10 Minutes A Day (3 books in 1 Box set)*
- **Author**: Rachel Mitchell
- **Year**: 2018
- **Length**: 335 pages
- **Format**: PDF, marketed as a self-study reference for IELTS Lexical Resource Band 8.0+

## Book structure

The book has two main parts:

1. **Topic sections** (pages 9–251): 24 thematic chapters, e.g. Education, Work, Health, Environment, Travel, Family.
2. **Other Vocabularies** (pages 252–325): an alphabetical reference of words/phrases that did not fit into a topic, ordered Letter A through Letter W (Letters J, Q, X, Y, Z are absent in the source).

Each entry follows a uniform format:

```
Headword/phrase [Part of Speech] (definition).
Example sentence 1.
Example sentence 2.
(occasionally a third example)
```

Parts of speech used in the source: **Noun**, **Verb**, **Adjective**, **Adverb**, **Expression**, **Conjunction**.
